Indian Chai

Description

This delightfully spiced tea uses cardamom, cinnamon and pepper for flavor. Stir in milk to taste.

Submitted by Margaret Thomas

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ quarts water
  • 1 quarts Milk
  • Four Black Tea Bags
  • 3 Tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 cinnamon stick, broken in half

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, bring water to a boil. Add the tea bags, cardamom seeds, cinnamon, and black pepper, then return to a boil.
  2. Remove pot from heat and let stand for 5 minutes.
  3. Serve and Drink while it is warm
